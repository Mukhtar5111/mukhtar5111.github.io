About Dinar Agricultural

Your trusted partner in agricultural machinery and processing equipment

Our Story

Founded in 2008, Dinar Agricultural began as a small spare parts supplier in Kano, Nigeria. Over the years, we've grown into a leading provider of agricultural machinery and processing equipment across West Africa.

Our Specialization

We specialize in:

  • Farm machinery and implements
  • Rice milling equipment
  • Flour milling machines
  • Genuine spare parts
  • Technical support and maintenance
